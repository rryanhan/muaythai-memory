# Clinch Knee Shield To Turn

## Summary
Use a knee shield inside the clinch to block pressure, then turn the partner as their weight shifts.

## Steps
1. Start in light clinch with partner applying forward pressure.
2. Bring knee shield inside to create space at the hip line.
3. Keep upper-body frame or collar tie stable.
4. As partner adjusts, step and turn them to the open side.
5. Reset with posture before throwing any knee.

## Notes
The shield creates the turn; do not just shove with the arms.
