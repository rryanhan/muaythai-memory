# Switch Step Lead Kick Off Jab

## Summary
Use the jab to hide the switch step and land the lead body kick without telegraphing.

## Steps
1. Throw a relaxed jab while staying tall.
2. As the jab retracts, switch the feet quietly.
3. Turn the hip over into the lead body kick.
4. Land balanced and bring hands back to guard.
5. Repeat with occasional fake switch to keep rhythm honest.

## Notes
The switch should not be louder than the kick.
