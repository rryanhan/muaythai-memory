# Bag Round Four Count Low Kick Rhythm

## Summary
A heavy bag rhythm drill to blend hands and low kicks under fatigue.

## Steps
1. Start with jab-cross to the bag.
2. Add rear low kick after the cross.
3. Reset and throw cross-hook-rear low kick.
4. Every fourth sequence, teep the bag to manage distance.
5. Keep the round continuous for three minutes.

## Notes
The bag should move, but the feet should not chase it blindly.
