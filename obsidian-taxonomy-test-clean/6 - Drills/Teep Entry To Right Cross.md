# Teep Entry To Right Cross

## Summary
Use the lead teep to freeze or move the opponent, then step in behind the retracting leg to land the cross.

## Steps
1. Show the lead teep from long range.
2. Touch the body or hip with the teep, focusing on balance over power.
3. Retract the leg straight back under the hip.
4. As the opponent settles, step in and throw the right cross.
5. Exit to the lead side or follow with left hook if the pad holder stays shelled.

## Notes
This should feel like the teep opens the door, not like two separate attacks.
