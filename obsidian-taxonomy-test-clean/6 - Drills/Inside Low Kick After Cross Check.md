# Inside Low Kick After Cross Check

## Summary
Use the cross to bring weight onto the opponent lead leg, then chop the inside low kick before they step out.

## Steps
1. Throw a straight right cross down the middle.
2. Let the cross step land slightly outside.
3. As their guard reacts, turn the hip into the inside low kick.
4. Recover the kicking leg under the body.
5. Reset or follow with jab to prevent the counter.

## Notes
Works best when the cross makes them brace on the lead leg.
