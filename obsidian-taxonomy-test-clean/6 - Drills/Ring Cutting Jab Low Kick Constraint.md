# Ring Cutting Jab Low Kick Constraint

## Summary
Constraint sparring where one partner practices cutting space with jab and low kick while the other tries to circle out.

## Steps
1. Partner A pressures at light contact.
2. Partner B circles and exits without throwing heavy counters.
3. Partner A may use jab, feint, and low kick only.
4. Goal is to step across the escape path, not chase.
5. Switch roles every round.

## Notes
This is a footwork drill disguised as sparring.
