# Jab Cross Rear Kick Pad Return

## Summary
Basic pad sequence that links straight punches to a rear round kick without pausing between hand range and kicking range.

## Steps
1. Start in stance with pad holder giving a light forward walk.
2. Throw jab-cross down the middle.
3. As the cross retracts, let the rear hip load without stepping too wide.
4. Throw the rear kick to body-height pad.
5. Recover the kicking leg under the body and reset guard before moving.

## Notes
Pad holder should punish lazy recovery with a touch jab so the reset matters.
