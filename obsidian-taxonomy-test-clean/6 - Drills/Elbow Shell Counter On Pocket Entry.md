# Elbow Shell Counter On Pocket Entry

## Summary
Shell against a short punching entry, then answer with a compact elbow as the opponent stays in the pocket.

## Steps
1. Partner steps in with light jab-cross or cross-hook.
2. Shell tight without dropping head blindly.
3. Feel the second punch land on guard.
4. Step in half a beat and throw compact elbow to pad or shoulder target.
5. Frame and exit.

## Notes
This is controlled. No live elbow contact in normal sparring.
