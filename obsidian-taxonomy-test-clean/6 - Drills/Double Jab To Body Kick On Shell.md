# Double Jab To Body Kick On Shell

## Summary
Draw the guard high with double jab pressure, then punish the covered side with a body kick.

## Steps
1. Throw the first jab to measure distance.
2. Throw the second jab harder to bring the guard up.
3. As the partner shells, step slightly outside.
4. Throw the rear body kick into the open side.
5. Recover with guard high and exit before the counter cross.

## Notes
The second jab is the trigger. If it does not move the guard, the kick is forced.
