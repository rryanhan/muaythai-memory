# Bag Drill Three Teeps And Angle Out

## Summary
A bag drill for building teep balance and learning not to stand in front after long-range strikes.

## Steps
1. Throw lead teep to the bag.
2. Throw rear teep to the bag.
3. Throw lead teep again as the bag returns.
4. Land and angle out to either side.
5. Reset before the bag swings back into you.

## Notes
Good for making teep practice less static.
