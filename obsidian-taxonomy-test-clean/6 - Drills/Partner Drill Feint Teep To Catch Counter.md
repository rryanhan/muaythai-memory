# Partner Drill Feint Teep To Catch Counter

## Summary
Feint the teep to draw a catch reaction, then punish the reaching hands with a punch or kick counter.

## Steps
1. Show the lead teep slowly enough for partner to react.
2. Partner reaches as if trying to catch.
3. Retract before contact and plant the foot.
4. Throw cross, hook, or low kick depending on their reach.
5. Reset and switch roles.

## Notes
This tests whether the feint creates a real reaction or just adds movement.
