Slip Right Step-Through Uppercut Exit

Pad Work

A counter drill for slipping the cross, stepping through, firing the uppercut, and exiting off-line.

Counter Rotation Head Movement Uppercut Exit Step

Steps

  1. Partner feeds jab-cross at controlled speed.
  2. Slip right and step through outside the centerline.
  3. Rotate back through the floor and fire the uppercut.
  4. Exit off-line instead of staying in the pocket.
