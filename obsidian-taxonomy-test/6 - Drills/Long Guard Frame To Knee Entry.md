# Long Guard Frame To Knee Entry

## Summary
Use a long guard frame to stop forward pressure and turn it into a knee entry before the opponent can clinch cleanly.

## Steps
1. Partner pressures forward with hands high.
2. Build a long guard frame across shoulder or collarbone line.
3. Keep rear hand ready to protect from the hook.
4. Step the rear foot in and throw a long knee to the body line.
5. Recover into collar tie, frame, or exit depending on partner reaction.

## Notes
The frame should create posture first. Knee only after their chest stops driving.
