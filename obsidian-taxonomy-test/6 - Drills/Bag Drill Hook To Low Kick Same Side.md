# Bag Drill Hook To Low Kick Same Side

## Summary
Use the lead hook to shift weight and set up the same-side low kick on the bag.

## Steps
1. Stand close enough for lead hook range.
2. Throw lead hook and let the hip rotate.
3. As the hook retracts, step or sit into lead low kick.
4. Recover into guard without drifting square.
5. Alternate sides every five reps.

## Notes
The hook and low kick should feel connected through the hip.
