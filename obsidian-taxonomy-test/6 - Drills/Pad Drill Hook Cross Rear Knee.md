# Pad Drill Hook Cross Rear Knee

## Summary
Short pad sequence that uses hook-cross to close the distance for a rear knee.

## Steps
1. Pad holder steps in with mitts high.
2. Throw lead hook to turn the guard.
3. Throw cross down the middle.
4. Step the rear foot in and throw rear knee to belly pad.
5. Pad holder gives light shove so fighter recovers stance.

## Notes
Great late in rounds because it forces hands-to-knees transition.
