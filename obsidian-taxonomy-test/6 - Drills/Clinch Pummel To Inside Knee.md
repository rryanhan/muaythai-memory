# Clinch Pummel To Inside Knee

## Summary
Pummel for inside position, stabilize posture, and throw a short inside knee without losing head position.

## Steps
1. Start chest-to-chest with both partners light on collar ties.
2. Pummel one arm inside to improve position.
3. Bring hips under the body and keep forehead pressure.
4. Throw a controlled inside knee to thigh or body line.
5. Return to base and continue pummeling.

## Notes
If the knee pulls you off balance, the clinch position was not stable enough.
