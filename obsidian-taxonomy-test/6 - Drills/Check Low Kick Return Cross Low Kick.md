# Check Low Kick Return Cross Low Kick

## Summary
Defend the low kick, land a quick cross as they reset, then return the low kick before they regain stance.

## Steps
1. Partner throws a light rear low kick.
2. Check with the lead shin and keep hands high.
3. As the kicking leg lands, step down into a right cross.
4. Immediately return the low kick to their lead leg.
5. Reset outside their punching line.

## Notes
Do not admire the check. The counter window is while their weight is settling.
