# Shadowbox Exit After Every Combination

## Summary
Shadowboxing constraint where every combination must finish with an angle, pivot, frame, or step-out.

## Steps
1. Start with any two-to-four strike combination.
2. After the final strike, immediately exit off the centerline.
3. Use a pivot, L-step, frame-and-step, or long step back at an angle.
4. Reset the guard before beginning the next combination.
5. Repeat for the full round without allowing a straight-back exit.

## Notes
Good for fixing the habit of finishing combinations in front of the opponent.
