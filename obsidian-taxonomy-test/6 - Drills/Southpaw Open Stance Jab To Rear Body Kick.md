# Southpaw Open Stance Jab To Rear Body Kick

## Summary
From southpaw/open stance, jab to occupy the lead hand and land the rear body kick across the open side.

## Steps
1. Start in southpaw or switch into southpaw.
2. Jab to the chest or face line.
3. Step slightly outside the opponent lead foot.
4. Throw the rear body kick across the open side.
5. Recover and exit away from their rear hand.

## Notes
The outside step matters more than kick power.
