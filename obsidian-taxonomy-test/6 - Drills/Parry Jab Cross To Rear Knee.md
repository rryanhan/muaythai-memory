# Parry Jab Cross To Rear Knee

## Summary
Parry the jab, counter cross to occupy the guard, then enter behind the cross with the rear knee.

## Steps
1. Partner feeds a light jab.
2. Parry across with the rear hand without overreaching.
3. Throw the cross immediately after the parry.
4. Step in and drive the rear knee to belly pad or body line.
5. Recover to clinch frame or exit.

## Notes
The parry should be small. Big parries expose the hook side.
