# Warmup Defensive Mirror Round

## Summary
A mirror-style warmup where one person leads defensive movement and the other copies to prime footwork and head movement.

## Steps
1. Partner A leads with slips, rolls, pivots, and small exits.
2. Partner B mirrors the movement without throwing strikes.
3. Switch leader every thirty seconds.
4. Keep stance width and guard consistent.
5. Add light jab feints in the final minute.

## Notes
Good before technical sparring because it wakes up defense without fatigue.
