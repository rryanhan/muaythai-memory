# Catch Body Kick Sweep To Angle Reset

## Summary
Catch a body kick, off-balance the partner, sweep or dump lightly, then reset at an angle instead of standing square.

## Steps
1. Partner throws a controlled body kick.
2. Catch with elbow tight and ribs covered.
3. Step slightly outside to avoid absorbing full force.
4. Pull the caught leg across while sweeping or steering their base leg.
5. Release safely and reset at an angle.

## Notes
Safety first. This is a balance and timing drill, not a hard takedown drill.
