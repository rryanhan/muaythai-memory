# Roll Under Hook To Right Kick

## Summary
Roll under the left hook and answer with a right kick as the partner turns past center.

## Steps
1. Partner throws jab-cross-left hook at light speed.
2. Slip or guard the jab-cross.
3. Roll under the hook, keeping eyes up.
4. As you come out, throw the right kick to body or leg.
5. Exit toward the side you rolled to.

## Notes
Do not roll too low. The goal is to make the hook miss and keep kicking posture.
