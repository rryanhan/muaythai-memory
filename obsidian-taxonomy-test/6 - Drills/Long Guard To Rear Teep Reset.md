# Long Guard To Rear Teep Reset

## Summary
Use long guard to slow pressure, then rear teep to create a clean reset instead of backing straight up.

## Steps
1. Partner walks forward behind light punches.
2. Build long guard and frame shoulder/chest line.
3. Take one angled step, not a straight retreat.
4. Fire rear teep to body line.
5. Reset stance and guard at long range.

## Notes
This is for people who get crowded and reach instead of managing distance.
