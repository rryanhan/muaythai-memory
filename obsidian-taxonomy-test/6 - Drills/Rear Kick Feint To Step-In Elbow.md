# Rear Kick Feint To Step-In Elbow

## Summary
Show the rear kick to lift the opponent posture or freeze the guard, then step in behind the feint for an elbow entry.

## Steps
1. Load the rear hip like a body kick.
2. Start the kick motion but do not turn fully over.
3. As the pad holder reacts, step the rear foot down closer.
4. Throw the step-in horizontal elbow or diagonal elbow to the pad.
5. Frame or exit immediately after the elbow.

## Notes
This needs commitment in the feint. Lazy hip show will not draw the reaction.
