# Lead Knee Off Hand Trap

## Summary
Trap or pin the lead hand, step in, and throw a lead knee before settling into clinch position.

## Steps
1. Partner keeps lead hand active in long guard.
2. Use rear hand to pin or redirect their lead wrist.
3. Step in with the rear foot to close distance.
4. Throw lead knee to body line or belly pad.
5. Land into frame or collar tie.

## Notes
The trap is brief. Hold it too long and the entry gets stiff.
