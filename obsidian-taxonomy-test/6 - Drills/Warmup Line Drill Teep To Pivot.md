# Warmup Line Drill Teep To Pivot

## Summary
A simple warmup line drill for teeping, landing balanced, and pivoting off the centerline.

## Steps
1. Move forward in stance across the mat.
2. Throw lead teep every three steps.
3. Retract the teep and land softly.
4. Pivot off the lead foot as if avoiding the counter.
5. Continue down the line and switch directions back.

## Notes
Useful before sparring days because it warms up range management and hips.
