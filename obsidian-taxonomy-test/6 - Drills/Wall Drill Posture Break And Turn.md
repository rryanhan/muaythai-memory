# Wall Drill Posture Break And Turn

## Summary
Use the wall as a constraint to practice breaking posture and turning the partner without muscling the clinch.

## Steps
1. Partner starts with back near wall or ropes.
2. Establish collar tie and bicep control.
3. Use a small pull-and-step to break posture.
4. Turn the partner off the wall by stepping across their base.
5. Reset and switch roles.

## Notes
The turn should come from foot position and posture, not just arm pulling.
